About Val

A creative professional with deep roots in storytelling, from theatre stages to documentary screens

Val's journey through the creative industries spans theatre production, dramaturgy, and documentary filmmaking. His work is characterized by narrative excellence, emotional intelligence, and an unwavering commitment to authentic storytelling. With over 15 years in documentary filmmaking and notable work aired on PBS, Val brings both artistic vision and practical expertise to every project.
