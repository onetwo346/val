About Arctic World Media

A creative production company with deep roots in storytelling, from theatre stages to documentary screens

Arctic World Media's journey through the creative industries spans theatre production, dramaturgy, and documentary filmmaking. Our work is characterized by narrative excellence, emotional intelligence, and an unwavering commitment to authentic storytelling. With over 15 years in documentary filmmaking and notable work aired on PBS, Arctic World Media brings both artistic vision and practical expertise to every project.

Arctic World Media's creative foundation was built in theatre, where our team worked as both producers and dramaturgs. This experience developed our expertise in:

  • Emotional Timing - Understanding the rhythm and pacing of powerful storytelling
  • Collaborative Storytelling - Working with creative teams to bring visions to life

Documentary Filmmaking

Arctic World Media transitioned into documentary filmmaking through pivotal collaborations with seasoned directors, launching a career that would span over a decade and a half.

This experience developed our expertise in:

  • Visual Storytelling - Creating compelling narratives through cinematography and editing
  • Interview Techniques - Drawing out authentic stories from subjects
  • Documentary Ethics - Balancing truth-telling with respect for subjects

These skills enable us to create powerful documentaries that resonate with audiences worldwide.
